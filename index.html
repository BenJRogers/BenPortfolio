About Me

Who I am

I’m a freelance developer, mainly specializing in react built applications. Ever since I built my first website I was hooked. I have a passion in designing intuitive user interfaces and then making them a reality with clean, future proof and reliable code. it's amazing seeing the apps I have built give someone enjoyment and help them in their daily lives and or business!
